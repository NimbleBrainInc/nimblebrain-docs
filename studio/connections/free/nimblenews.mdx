## What is NimbleNews?

NimbleNews provides access to news headlines and articles. Use it to:

- Monitor news about specific topics or companies
- Get daily news briefings
- Track industry trends
- Stay informed about current events

## Example Usage

### Daily News Briefing

```
Get the top 5 technology news headlines from today
```

### Company Monitoring

```
Search for news about Apple in the last 24 hours
```

### Industry Tracking

```
Find recent news about renewable energy and sustainability
```

### Event Monitoring

```
Every morning, check for news about our competitors and summarize any major announcements
```

## Limitations

- **Rate limits**: Reasonable request limits apply
- **Recency**: Best for current news (not historical archives)
- **Summary only**: Returns headlines and summaries, not full articles

## Example Automation

**Morning News Brief**

```
Every weekday at 7am:
1. Get top headlines in technology and finance
2. Search for news about [your company name]
3. Search for news about [competitor names]
4. Compile into a summary
5. Send via NimbleMail to the team
```
