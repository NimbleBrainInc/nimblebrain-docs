## Email Support

For account issues, billing questions, or private support inquiries, email us directly:

<Card
  title="support@nimblebrain.ai"
  icon="envelope"
  href="mailto:support@nimblebrain.ai"
>
  Send us an email and we'll get back to you.
</Card>

**Support hours:** Monday through Friday (business days)

**First response SLA:** Within 24 hours on business days

When emailing, please include:
- Your workspace name or account email
- A description of the issue or question
- Any relevant error messages or screenshots
- Steps to reproduce (for bugs)
